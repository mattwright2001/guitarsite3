MATT'S GUITAR

The guitar was originally meant to be played with Spanish Flaminco dancers. It has since evolved to be one of the most popular instruments in the western world.

Guitar Anatomy

Headstock

The headstock is the very top of the guitar where the tuners are positioned. This is where the strings connect with the rest of the guitar.

Neck and Frets

The neck is the long piece of wood connecting the headstock to the body of the guitar. On the anterior side of the guitar are the frets, the metal pieces that seperate what notes are being played.

Strings

The strings run along the length of the neck. When they are strummed or plucked, the vibration makes the sound.

Body

The body is the biggest part of the guitar. It is the large hollow box-like piece at the bottem of the guitar. Inside the body, the vibrations echo, amplifying the sound. The body is the most important part of the guitar.
